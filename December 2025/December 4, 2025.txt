Psalms 20-21, Luke 8, [Acts 15], Proverbs 4


RHEMA: Luke 8:15 NLT
And the seeds that fell on the good soil represent honest, good-hearted people who hear God’s word, cling to it, and patiently produce a huge harvest.

To produce huge harvest:
1. Good-hearted - heart check. always align your heart to what is good, 
2. Hear - listen to what God will say to you, dont mind what other says, focus on God's word
3. Cling - hold on to he promise of God, never let go
4. Patient - enjoy the process. sometimes it slow but surely you will reproduce a huge harvest


COMMAND: Luke 8:18 NLT
“So pay attention to how you hear. To those who listen to my teaching, more understanding will be given. But for those who are not listening, even what they think they understand will be taken away from them.”
