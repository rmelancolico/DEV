Acts 8:21 NIV
You have no part or share in this ministry, because your heart is not right before God.

* in doing the ministry that God entrusted to you, your heart matters most. You need to put your heart into it. Your heart should be in the right disposition. Doing the ministry with the wrong heart will lead to burnouts, and will disqualify your inheritance. Always check your heart!

Heart check checklist
- it is for God's glory and not for self gain
- it is lead by the Holy Spirit
- it is confirm by the gospel
- it result in obedience through faith
