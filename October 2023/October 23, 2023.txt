Genesis 16, Proverbs 23, John 9


RHEMA: Proverbs 23:4-5 NIV
Do not wear yourself out to get rich; do not trust your own cleverness. Cast but a glance at riches, and they are gone, for they will surely sprout wings and fly off to the sky like an eagle.


REFLECTION:

Dili ko masoya sa akong classmate na prosperous na. Dili dapat ko mag hina hina na maningkamot sa kaugalingon kusog para mo datu. Seek God first is the key. Dapat mag focus ko sa building disciples.
